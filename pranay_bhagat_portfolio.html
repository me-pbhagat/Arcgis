Flood Mapping
Bagmati River Flood Hazard Assessment

Comprehensive flood inundation mapping for a 45 km reach of the Bagmati River using 10 m SRTM DEM, 50-year and 100-year return period flows, and HEC-RAS 2D modelling integrated in ArcGIS Pro.

  • Delineated high, medium, and low hazard zones across 3 municipalities
  • Produced depth-velocity and flood extent maps at 1:25,000 scale
  • Delivered GIS layers for district disaster management plan
ArcGIS Pro HEC-RAS 2D SRTM DEM HEC-HMS
⚡ Hydropower GIS
Hydropower Design
6 MW Run-of-River Hydropower Layout

Full GIS-based feasibility layout for a 6 MW run-of-river scheme in Sindhupalchok district: intake siting on 1:10,000 topo sheets, canal alignment optimization, forebay location, and penstock profile extraction with slope analysis.

  • Identified optimal intake elevation to maximize net head (218 m)
  • Minimized excavation cost by 15% through GIS slope-based routing
  • Produced AutoCAD-ready alignment for detailed design phase
ArcGIS Pro Contour Analysis AutoCAD Global Mapper
🏔 Watershed Analysis
Catchment & Hydrology
Trishuli Sub-basin Catchment Delineation

Multi-catchment delineation of a 1,200 km² sub-basin using ALOS PALSAR 12.5 m DEM, ArcHydro toolset, and SCS-CN runoff method. Developed design flood hydrographs for 10, 25, 50, and 100 year return periods for bridge scour analysis.

  • Delineated 14 sub-catchments with drainage area calculation
  • Computed Q100 peak discharge of 1,340 m³/s for bridge design
  • Submitted as part of Master's thesis with distinction
ArcHydro ALOS PALSAR SCS-CN HEC-HMS
